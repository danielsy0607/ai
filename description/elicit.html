About Elicit AI

Elicit is an AI research assistant designed to streamline literature reviews, gather relevant papers, and extract critical information efficiently. It uses advanced AI to help researchers summarize findings, generate insights, and stay up-to-date with scientific research.

Ideal for academics, students, and professionals, Elicit saves time and improves research quality by automating tedious tasks like screening papers and synthesizing evidence.

Key Features

  • AI-assisted literature search and screening
  • Extraction of key findings and insights from papers
  • Automated summarization of research articles
  • Supports systematic reviews and evidence synthesis
  • Helps organize research efficiently with intuitive workflows
